Succulent Match, is a memory game I have made for my course project for Code Institue in order to demonstrate my javascript knowledge.

I chose succulents as the subject matter for my project as I am passionate about these plants and the many different varieties you can find.

As you will see, through playing the game, succulents can vary in shape, size and colour. This is the reason I find them very interesting and have started my own succulent collection.

Hopefully, through this game, I will spark an interest in these strange but interesting plants for others as well!

How to play...

The object of the game is to find the hidden pairs in the quickest time, the most accurate and smallest number of moves

You will get to reveal 2 cards of your choice per 'move' to see if they match

Ready to play?

There are 3 levels of difficulty for the game:

  • Easy = 6 pairs to find
  • Medium = 8 pairs to find
  • Hard = 10 pairs to find

Game statistics on number of moves and accuracy are shown during the game and the completed time will be shown once all cards have been matched

Try to beat your time or accuracy with each game you play

Good luck!
